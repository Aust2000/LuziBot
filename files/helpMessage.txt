Commands List (prefix "luzi")

- "help": Shows this message.
- "hello": Introducts itself.
- "ping": Tells the latency.
- "say": Says something you type.
- "8ball": Awnsers a yer or no question.
- "vote": Write an statement. People can vote through the reactions to choose yes or no.
- "delete": Deletes a determined amount of messages.
- "kick": Kicks someone. Type the person's @ after the command. You can optionally give the reason for the kick after the @. (off)
- "ban": Bans someone. Type the person's @ after the command. You can optionally give the reason for the ban after the @. (off)
- "config": Changes some of the bot's configurations. To get more info, type "luzi helpie config".
